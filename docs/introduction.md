# Welcome to Playgrounds.dev

Playgrounds.dev gives your team instant, isolated full-stack cloud environments powered by Docker.

## What you can do

- **Launch environments** — Spin up complete stacks with a single click
- **Share previews** — Every environment gets a unique subdomain
- **Iterate faster** — No more waiting for staging pipelines
